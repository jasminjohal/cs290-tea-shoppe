About

Jasmin's Tea Shoppe is the premier milk tea shop in Corvallis, OR. It was established in 2019 to meet Oregonians' specific tea needs. We pride ourselves in our emphasis on customization. We ensure all of our ingredients are high quality but the composition of the tea is left up to the customer! Select each of the following according to your personal preference:

  • Tea
  • Milk
  • Sweetness
  • Topping

We are open daily. Stop by to get your perfect cup!
